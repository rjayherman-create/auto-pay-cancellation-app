## 📊 EMOTION PARAMETERS EXPLAINED

### Pitch Shift
- **Range:** 0.5x - 2.0x
- **Low (0.5x):** Deep, serious, sad
- **Normal (1.0x):** Neutral baseline
- **High (2.0x):** Excited, shocked, playful

### Speed Modifier
- **Range:** 0.5x - 2.0x
- **Slow (0.75x):** Thoughtful, serious, emotional
- **Normal (1.0x):** Conversational
- **Fast (1.3x):** Energetic, excited, scared

### Volume Shift
- **Range:** 0.5x - 2.0x
- **Quiet (0.7x):** Intimate, scared, sad
- **Normal (1.0x):** Regular speaking
- **Loud (1.4x):** Angry, emphasized, commanding

### Breathiness
- **Range:** 0% - 100%
- **0%:** Solid, confident, strong
- **50%:** Natural, comfortable
- **100%:** Vulnerable, intimate, scared

### Resonance Type
- **Bright:** Happy, excited, young
- **Warm:** Romantic, caring, friendly
- **Deep:** Serious, authoritative, villain
- **Hollow:** Sad, lost, empty
- **Thin:** Scared, weak, uncertain

## 💡 PROFESSIONAL TIPS

### Tip 1: Emotion Intensity
- **0.3 - 0.5:** Subtle, natural emotion
- **0.5 - 0.8:** Moderate, noticeable emotion
- **0.8 - 1.0:** Strong, clear emotion
- **1.0 - 1.5:** Extreme, theatrical emotion

### Tip 2: Blending for Complexity
Good blends to try:
- **Happy + Playful:** Bouncy, fun energy
- **Angry + Serious:** Controlled fury
- **Sad + Sympathetic:** Deep empathy
- **Confident + Determined:** Unwavering resolve
- **Romantic + Sympathetic:** Deep caring

### Tip 3: Character Consistency
- Keep hero voice consistent across scenes
- Use same emotion settings for character continuity
- Vary intensity but keep base personality

### Tip 4: Scene Transitions
- Use emotion blending for transitions
- Gradually shift from one emotion to another
- Creates natural character arc

### Tip 5: Combine Techniques
For maximum impact, combine:
- Emotion base (e.g., happy)
- Vocal technique (e.g., vibrato)
- Prosody customization
- Sound effect layering

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem: Emotion not applying
**Solution:** Ensure text field filled, select emotion, click apply button

### Problem: Blend feels off
**Solution:** Try different blend ratios (0.3-0.7 often work best)

### Problem: Character voice sounds wrong
**Solution:** Try different scene to match emotional context

### Problem: Too much emotion
**Solution:** Reduce intensity slider (try 0.5-0.7)

### Problem: Not enough emotion
**Solution:** Increase intensity slider (try 1.2-1.5)
