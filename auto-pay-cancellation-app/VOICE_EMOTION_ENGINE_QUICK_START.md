## 🎯 3-Tab System

### Tab 1: 💙 Apply Emotion
- Select emotion
- Enter text
- Adjust intensity (0.3 to 1.5)
- Click apply
- ✅ Done!

### Tab 2: 🎨 Blend Emotions

## 📊 Emotion Parameters

Every emotion has:
- **Pitch:** High (excited) or Low (serious)
- **Speed:** Fast (energetic) or Slow (thoughtful)
- **Volume:** Loud (commanding) or Quiet (intimate)
- **Breathiness:** Shows vulnerability/intimacy
- **Resonance:** Voice color (bright, warm, deep, hollow)
