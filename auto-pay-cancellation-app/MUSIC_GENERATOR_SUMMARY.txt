================================================================================
SETUP:
================================================================================

Add API keys to .env:

SUNO_API_KEY=your-key-here
UNETIC_API_KEY=your-key-here
AUDIOCRAFT_API_KEY=your-key-here

Restart backend:
docker compose down && docker compose up

================================================================================
QUICK START:
================================================================================

1. Open http://localhost:8000
2. Click "Music Generator" card
3. Describe: "upbeat electronic with strong bass"
4. Duration: 30 seconds
5. Genre: Electronic
6. Click "Generate Music"
7. Wait 10-60 seconds
8. Preview and download
