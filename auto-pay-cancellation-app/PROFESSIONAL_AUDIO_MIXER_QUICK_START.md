## 🎯 Add Your Audio

### Step 1: Add Track
```
1. Click "🎚️ Mixer"
2. Select type: Voiceover / Music / SFX / Ambience
3. Enter name: "Hero Voiceover"
4. Set duration: 30 seconds
5. Click "➕ Add Track"
```

### Step 2: Control Volume
Use the volume slider for each track:
- Voiceover: 1.0 (100%)
- Music: 0.7 (70%) 
- Effects: 0.5 (50%)
- Ambience: 0.3 (30%)

### Step 3: Add Effects
Click "⚡ Effects & EQ" to see presets

### Step 4: Export
Click "💾 Export Mix" to download

## 🎚️ Track Types

| Type | Volume | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| Voiceover | 1.0 | Dialogue/narration |
| Music | 0.7 | Background |
| SFX | 0.5 | Impact/transition |
| Ambience | 0.3 | Background layer |

---

## 🎛️ EQ Quick Reference

**For Voiceover:**
- Bass: -2
- Mid: +2
- Treble: +1
→ Makes dialogue clear & present

**For Music:**
- Bass: +1
- Mid: 0
- Treble: +3
→ Brightens music

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ Start with volume sliders first
✅ Use EQ presets for your track type
✅ Listen on multiple speakers/headphones
✅ Don't over-compress
✅ Leave headroom (-3dB to -6dB)
✅ Reference with other mixes

## 🎬 Quick Mix Example

**Cartoon Scene (30 seconds)**

```
Track 1: Hero Voiceover
- Volume: 1.0
- EQ: Dialogue Clear preset
- Add: Fade in 0.5s, Fade out 0.5s

Track 2: Background Music  
- Volume: 0.7
- EQ: Music Bright preset
- Add: Fade in 1.0s, Fade out 1.0s

Track 3: Jump Sound Effect
- Volume: 0.5
- Pan: Center
- Add: Short reverb

Master:
- Gain: 0.9
- Compression: Medium preset
- EQ: Flat (no change)

Export: 30-second polished mix
```
